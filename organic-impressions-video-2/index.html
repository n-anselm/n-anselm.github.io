Organic Impressions - Video

Back in April (2023) I went to a local park in the city with the intent of testing some new camera gear. The result is this somewhat abstract video that I put together. It wasn't exactly planned well and didn't turn out perfect, it was more about just making something.

This was shot with the Sony A7iii, a 35mm f1.8 lens, and DJI RSC 2 gimbal. Edited in DaVinci Resolve. Enjoy!
